Our Rescue Services

Search & Rescue Dogs

Our trained dogs assist in natural disasters and missing person cases, using their keen sense of smell and obedience training to locate and aid individuals in crisis.

Support Monkeys

Monkeys are trained to retieve items, operate tools, and support individuals with mobility impairments in high-stress or emergency situations.

Community Outreach

We provide therapy animal visits, educational programs for schools, and hands on volunteer oppurtunities to build public awareness and compassion.
