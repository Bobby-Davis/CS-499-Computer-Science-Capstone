About Us

At Grazioso Salvare, our mission is to train and support exceptional rescue animals who assist in search and rescue, emotional support, and medical alert roles.

Our team begain with a small group of handlers and volunteers who believed in second chances for both animal and people. Over the years, we've grown into a nationwide network of trainers, veterinarians, and support specialists dedicated to service through rescue.

We believe in compassion, skill, and the unbreakable bond between humans and animals. Every dog, monkey, and rescue animal we train helps create a safer, more connected world.
